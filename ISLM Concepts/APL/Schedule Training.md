># Schedule training

You can schedule a training for your intelligent scenarios. Once the trainings are scheduled, model versions are created automatically based on the selected frequency and preselected filters.

1. Open the Fiori Launchpad by clicking [here](https://18.214.3.29:44301/sap/bc/ui5_ui5/ui2/ushell/shells/abap/FioriLaunchpad.html?sap-client=100&sap-language=EN#Shell-home). Click on the **Intelligent Scenario Management** app. 
<img width="1100" height="357" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6a7bfdec-9da0-4472-b5ca-682e4c7d4022" />

2. Search the **First-Class Seats Occupied** scenario created by you and navigate to the details page by clicking the **>** icon. 
<img width="1838" height="399" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f0d0097a-bf3e-468a-986f-807cc50faa2d" />

3. Click the **Create Schedule** button. 
<img width="1792" height="425" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/19f96bc0-423f-459b-92d5-1bde3781542c" />

4. View the information displayed in the screen. Click on **Model Version** value help. 
<img width="1835" height="764" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5376e395-316e-4281-9125-6df1660eab3a" />

5. Select the reference Model Version. 
<img width="1713" height="754" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c55bdd47-4e35-4ca6-9118-ad513c03c4cb" />

6. Click on **Go to Step 2**. 
<img width="1798" height="757" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d3fea32f-c2e4-44a6-b691-5569b0fa2e2c" />

7. Review Scheduling Options automatically proposed by the system.

8. Enter the Start as **2 minutes later** than the system proposed time.

9. Schedule for **1 Occurrence(s)** and click on **Go to Step 3**. 
<img width="1346" height="610" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a2286734-2201-43a1-8c49-00b99bf110a6" />

10. Review the section Intelligent Scenarios Details and Schedule Details. Click the **Create Schedule** button. 
<img width="1890" height="931" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a13ea8f9-e4e3-4da5-a782-ec32d9c2b9d2" />

11. Click on **Schedule Planned Task** tab to view the planned task created as a result of scheduling of training. Please wait until the given start time of the task and status is completed. 
<img width="1783" height="436" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0bc180ed-6ed7-4382-99e3-9e2362f2639d" />

12. Once the status changes to **Completed**, click the **>** button. 
<img width="1795" height="433" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d0046629-a846-4164-9951-d7f630ff7631" />

13. Review **scheduling Options** and **Scheduling Runs** tab. 
<img width="1461" height="627" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/766aafa7-c142-486d-aec2-dc74c3c9c611" />

14. Click the highlighted icon to view the created Model Version.
**Note: This step will take time** as logs are opened in a dialog. 
<img width="1461" height="627" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff58fc84-ec3b-4cd6-9376-ed761f07c45c" />

15. View the created **Model Version ID**. 
<img width="1724" height="386" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2bba9185-d74f-46c1-b4af-cb37ffefcce2" />

16. Close the **Application log** dialog and to navigate back Scheduled Planned Tasks screen by clicking <img width="106" height="35" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f1a25d00-8574-4a75-ae96-0ef5931feb6b" />. Select the **Models** tab and Click on **>** icon to view the created Model Version. 
<img width="1785" height="416" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc4a35af-c4b0-47ac-b71a-33d60023fc4b" />

17. View the created Model Version as outcome of Scheduling of Training. 
<img width="1919" height="678" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8051c850-ac17-49f8-9c46-588605d68d76" />

**Well done, you just scheduled training.**

🎉 Congratulations! 🎉 You have successfully completed the exercise.
