># Operate the Intelligent Scenario

Once the Intelligent Scenario is published, the Intelligent Scenario Management app helps you to deploy and activate the model for productive usage. In this section, you will use the **Intelligent Scenario Management app** to perform operations on your Intelligent Scenario.

1. Open the Fiori Launchpad by clicking here. Click on **Intelligent Scenario Management section** and choose the **Intelligent Scenario Management app**.
 <img width="1100" height="357" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bea91086-5eed-4371-93d8-c713dc1d20c4" />

2. Search the **Gen AI** scenario created by you and navigate to the details page by clicking the **>** icon.
   <img width="1869" height="591" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fdc6ccab-2ea2-4ffc-b9ca-27112a8f1320" />

3. Navigate to details page by clicking the **>** icon
   <img width="1869" height="591" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c1909f2c-087f-4adb-a3b7-f33a57dbc8d9" />

4. Select the default training and click the **Deploy** Button.
   <img width="1904" height="664" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/951f47dd-02b0-453a-bb5d-2b3f2922fe24" />

5. Click the **Deploy and Monitor** Button.
   <img width="1725" height="963" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b2c0c502-e617-451c-ac9f-ad4405c351b8" />

6. A new Deployment will be created in **Deployment Pending status**.
   <img width="1725" height="963" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/16a991bc-e813-4d00-992c-62760c21cadf" />

7. Monitor the status of Deployment and check the status changes to Deployed.

   **Note:** Deployments can take approximately 10 minutes to be Deployed.
   <img width="1898" height="720" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/60d91b5f-b007-4d2e-bb9a-6b7640ead45d" />

9. Activate the deployment to run inference. Select the Deployment and click on **Activate** button and choose **For All** option.
   <img width="1896" height="660" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/00da79a0-ec84-4e3f-bec9-f9f6cdb7b03e" />

10. In the dialog Activate for All Users, choose **Activate For All** button.
    <img width="720" height="205" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c685a42f-7c3b-4ecb-b71a-5d100c2c1267" />

Well done, you just Used Intelligent Scenario Management app to deploy and activate the llm model.
