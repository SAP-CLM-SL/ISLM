### Alpha Return Policy
**Last Updated: January 15, 2026**

Thank you for shopping with Alpha. We want you to love your purchase. If not, returns are accepted within **30 days** for a full refund or exchange.

**Return Window**  
Items must be returned within 30 days from delivery date. Extensions for holidays and defective items.

**Eligibility**  
- Items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags attached.  
- Proof of purchase required (receipt or order number; order history accepted as alternative).  
- Condition: Unworn and unwashed.

**Non-Returnable Items**  
- Software, undergarments, perishables, custom products, final sale items.  
- Reasons: Hygiene, personalized nature, or opened seals.

**Return Process**  
- Initiate via online portal or email support.  
- Steps: 1. Log into account. 2. Select return reason. 3. Print label. 4. Ship back.  
- Return authorization required; form at returns.alpha.com; prepaid label provided.  
- Shipping: Customer pays return shipping; use UPS or FedEx. Address: Alpha Returns, 123 Main St, Austin, TX 78701.  
- Required documents: Receipt, packing slip.

**Refunds**  
- Types: Full refund, exchange, store credit.  
- Default: Full refund to original payment.  
- Processing: 5-7 business days.  
- Fees: 10% restocking fee ($5 min); applies to apparel; other fees: none.

**Shipping Costs**  
Original shipping not refunded. Return shipping not refunded.

**Special Cases**  
- Defective/damaged: Extended window to 60 days, free shipping, full refund.  
- Wrong item: Expedited exchange at no cost.  
- Holidays: Yes, extensions apply.  
- Other: Gift returns without receipt get store credit.

**Contact**  
Email: support@alpha.com | Phone: 1-800-123-4567 | Live chat: Yes | Return center: returns.alpha.com

**Legal Notes**  
- Complies with state laws in TX and CA.  
- Warranties: 1-year manufacturer warranty on electronics.
