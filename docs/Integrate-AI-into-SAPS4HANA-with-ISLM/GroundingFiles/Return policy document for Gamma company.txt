### Gamma Return Policy
**Last Updated: December 10, 2025**

Gamma prioritizes satisfaction with flexible options. No returns on sale items.

**Return Window**  
14 days from delivery. No extensions.

**Eligibility**  
- Conditions: Unused, tags on.  
- Proof: Not required (order history auto-looked up).  
- Condition: Pristine, unopened.

**Non-Returnable**  
- Perishables, custom products, final sale, software.  
- Reasons: Spoilage risk, made-to-order.

**Process**  
- Initiation: Email support or phone.  
- Steps: 1. Email photo. 2. Get approval. 3. Ship.  
- Authorization: Required; no form URL; label not provided.  
- Shipping: Customer pays; pack securely. Address: Gamma Returns Dept, 789 Pine Rd, Miami, FL 33101. Carriers: Any.  
- Documents: Packing slip, photo of issue.

**Refunds**  
- Types: Partial refund, exchange.  
- Default: Exchange.  
- Payment: Gift card only.  
- Time: 7-10 days.  
- Fees: $15 restocking (15%); applies to electronics; other: Return shipping.

**Shipping Costs**  
Original: False. Return: False.

**Special Cases**  
- Defective: No extended window, free shipping false, full refund true.  
- Wrong item: Process: Contact for correction.  
- Holidays: True.  
- Other: None.

**Contact**  
Email: help@gamma.com | Phone: None | Live chat: False | URL: None
